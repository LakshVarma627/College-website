About Us

Shaping Tomorrow's Engineers

Government Polytechnic Arvi is an NBA accredited, AICTE approved, and DTE recognized institution, affiliated with MSBTE. Our mission is to provide high-quality technical education that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and hands-on skills to empower students for successful careers in engineering and technology. The institution is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped labs, a modern library, and advanced classrooms, creating an ideal learning environment. Our experienced faculty is dedicated to combining theoretical knowledge with practical experience, ensuring students are industry-ready. We offer numerous opportunities for internships, workshops, and projects, focusing on both technical and soft skills to shape responsible professionals. With a vision to be a leading institution in technical education, we strive to produce skilled engineers who can innovate and contribute to the nation's growth.
